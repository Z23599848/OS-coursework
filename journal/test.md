Absolutely! Here’s a categorized list of useful math formulas across different topics:

---

### **1. Algebra**

* **Quadratic formula:**
  [
  x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}
  ]
* **Difference of squares:**
  [
  a^2 - b^2 = (a-b)(a+b)
  ]
* **Sum of cubes:**
  [
  a^3 + b^3 = (a+b)(a^2 - ab + b^2)
  ]
* **Difference of cubes:**
  [
  a^3 - b^3 = (a-b)(a^2 + ab + b^2)
  ]

---

### **2. Geometry**

* **Area of a triangle:**
  [
  A = \frac{1}{2} \times \text{base} \times \text{height}
  ]
* **Area of a circle:**
  [
  A = \pi r^2
  ]
* **Circumference of a circle:**
  [
  C = 2 \pi r
  ]
* **Pythagoras theorem:**
  [
  a^2 + b^2 = c^2
  ]

---

### **3. Trigonometry**

* **Sine, cosine, tangent:**
  [
  \sin \theta = \frac{\text{opposite}}{\text{hypotenuse}}, \quad
  \cos \theta = \frac{\text{adjacent}}{\text{hypotenuse}}, \quad
  \tan \theta = \frac{\text{opposite}}{\text{adjacent}}
  ]
* **Pythagorean identity:**
  [
  \sin^2 \theta + \cos^2 \theta = 1
  ]

---

### **4. Calculus**

* **Derivative of power function:**
  [
  \frac{d}{dx}[x^n] = nx^{n-1}
  ]
* **Integral of power function:**
  [
  \int x^n dx = \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1} + C \quad (n \neq -1)
  ]

---

### **5. Probability & Statistics**

* **Probability of an event:**
  [
  P(E) = \frac{\text{Number of favorable outcomes}}{\text{Total outcomes}}
  ]
* **Mean (average):**
  [
  \text{Mean} = \frac{\text{Sum of all values}}{\text{Number of values}}
  ]
